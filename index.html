Due to amendments in Health Ministry Order Nr. 59 Forvaters Terra is allowed to continue Medical examinations for seafarers. Medical examinations will be done only by appointment. Please be precise and arrive on time when you are coming to see doctor! You must wear a face mask! You must bring Seamen Book to prove being off board for more than 14 days. You must not come, if you need to quarantine or you have been in a contact with COVID-19 patient!!!

Video & Important to Know!

  1. Must bring Seaman’s Book (ID, or passport) and last Medical Certificate.
  2. If this is the first time (or interruption of 5+ years), must bring these references:
    • Form u/27 from Family Physician;
    • Reference from the Psychiatry Centre;
    • Reference from the Narcology Centre
  3. Must come on empty stomach or at least 2 hours after the last meal.
  4. In case of health problems (diabetes, blood pressure, etc.), must have references from attending doctor, that state the diagnose and reccommendations.
  5. In case of weight problems, BMI> 30, must pass endurance test.
    • What is my BMI?
  6. There is a possibility (extra 10EUR) to have laboratory results quicker (in 1 - 1.5h).
